A Glimpse into Life at Henley Care House

At Henley Care House, we strive to create a warm, welcoming, and engaging environment where our residents can truly feel at home. Life here is about comfort, companionship, and opportunities to enjoy each day to the fullest. We focus on promoting independence, well-being, and a sense of belonging within our close-knit community.

Daily Life and Activities

Every day at Henley Care House offers a blend of personal time, engaging activities, and social interaction. While respecting individual routines and preferences, we provide a framework that encourages activity and connection.

Comfortable Mornings

Residents can enjoy a relaxed start to their day, with assistance available for personal care and dressing, followed by a delicious breakfast served in our communal dining room or in the comfort of their own room.

Engaging Activities

Our dedicated activities coordinator organises a varied programme of events and activities tailored to residents' interests, including arts and crafts, gentle exercise, music therapy, quizzes, and social gatherings.

Nutritious Mealtimes

Lunch and dinner are important social occasions at Henley Care House. Our chefs prepare tasty and balanced meals, catering for dietary needs and preferences. Residents can dine together or have meals in their rooms.

Afternoon Relaxation

The afternoons offer opportunities for relaxation, reading, hobbies, or spending time in our comfortable lounges or gardens. Staff are always on hand for support and companionship.

Social Connections

We encourage social interaction and provide comfortable spaces for residents to connect with each other, build friendships, and enjoy a sense of community.

Peaceful Evenings

Evenings are a time for winding down, perhaps enjoying a light snack or watching television. Our care team provides support with evening routines to ensure a comfortable and peaceful night's sleep.

Our Comfortable Environment

Henley Care House is designed to provide a safe, comfortable, and homely environment for all our residents.

  • Well-appointed and comfortable bedrooms
  • Spacious and welcoming communal lounges
  • Accessible and secure gardens
  • En-suite facilities in many rooms
  • Nutritious and varied menu choices
  • Regular cleaning and laundry services
  • Safe and supportive environment
