About Henley Care House

Henley Care House is dedicated to providing a compassionate and supportive living environment for our residents, where their comfort, dignity, and well-being are our top priorities. We understand the importance of creating a true home, and we strive to make every day fulfilling and secure for those in our care.

Our care home has been established with a clear vision: to offer more than just accommodation, but a community where individuals are respected, encouraged, and receive personalised attention tailored to their unique needs.

Our Caring Team

At the heart of Henley Care House is our team of dedicated and empathetic caregivers. Our experienced care assistants, registered nurses, and activities coordinators work collaboratively to ensure the physical, emotional, and social needs of our residents are met with the utmost professionalism and warmth.

We invest in the ongoing training and development of our staff, empowering them with the skills and understanding to deliver person-centred care. We take pride in fostering a supportive and respectful environment, both for our residents and our team.

Our Approach to Care

Our approach to care is centred around the individual. We take the time to get to know each resident, understanding their life history, preferences, and specific care requirements. This enables us to develop tailored care plans that promote independence, dignity, and a sense of purpose.

We believe in open communication and actively involve families in the care planning process, ensuring that their insights and wishes are valued and integrated into the care we provide. Together, we create a supportive network that enhances the lives of our residents.
