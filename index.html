Why Choose Henley Care House?

Henley Care House is more than just a care home; it's a community where residents feel safe, supported, and cherished. Our experienced and compassionate team is committed to providing person-centered care, ensuring that each individual's unique needs and preferences are met with the utmost attention.

We offer a range of high-quality services, from comfortable residential care to specialized dementia and nursing care. Our aim is to create a warm and stimulating environment where residents can thrive, enjoy fulfilling days, and maintain their independence as much as possible.

Our Retirement Apartments offer an independent living option with the peace of mind of having care services available if needed. For families needing short-term support, our Respite Care services provide a safe and comfortable environment for their loved ones.

Our Personal Approach at Henley Care House

At Henley Care House, we understand that welcoming someone new into your life, or placing your loved one in care, is a significant step. That's why we take a very careful and considerate approach to matching our residents with the right care and support.

Our comprehensive assessment and matching process is overseen by our dedicated Care Coordinator. They will take the time to understand individual needs, preferences, hobbies, and interests. This thorough approach ensures that the care and companionship provided at Henley Care House are not just professional, but also genuinely compatible.

Once a resident settles into Henley Care House, they will receive ongoing support from their dedicated care team. This includes regular check-ins and a consistent point of contact within our professional team, ensuring a smooth and comfortable experience for everyone.
